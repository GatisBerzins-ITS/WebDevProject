18th April 2021

Today was the 3rd day I worked over at the Bakery. I've certainly made an improvement (in terms of speed) when comparing to the last day I worked. I spent an hour alone memorising different slices/cakes that the bakery makes and ships out to different stores in the area.
I work as a Bakery Packer. These are the duties of a Bakery Packer.
  1. Cleaning Packaging materials
  2. Loading items into containers
  3. Labelling items correctly
  4. Making sure each client gets exactly what they ordered
  5. Checking for defective goods
